About Me

As a child, I never found books interesting. In fact, I would do anything to avoid reading. However, that changed during my senior year of high school. One day, out of curiosity, I picked up a book and started reading. That moment changed everything.

The first book that truly captivated me was *The Alchemist* by Paulo Coelho. Its themes of self-discovery and adventure spoke to me in ways I never expected. After finishing it, I realized that reading was a lot more than words on paper.

Today, reading is a big part of my life. I set reading goals each year and challenge myself to explore new genres. My ultimate dream is to build my own personal library filled with books that have shaped my perspective.
